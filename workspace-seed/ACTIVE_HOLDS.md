## What This Is

Temporary context filters that affect how I respond and what I prioritize:
- They have expiry dates
- They get reviewed regularly
- They shape interpretation of all other context
- When they expire, they drop unless renewed

## Example

### Work Project Crunch

- **What:** User has major deadline in 2 weeks
- **Why:** All other goals secondary until this is done
- **Set:** 2026-02-15
- **Expires:** 2026-03-01
- **Release when:** Project submitted successfully

**Impact:**
- Deprioritize other projects
- Focus check-ins on progress
- Don't suggest new initiatives
- Celebrate small wins to maintain momentum

## Review Process

Every weekly review:
1. Check all active holds
2. Renew if still relevant
3. Let expire if resolved
4. Archive expired holds
