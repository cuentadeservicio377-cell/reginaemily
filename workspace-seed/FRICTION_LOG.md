# FRICTION_LOG.md - Contradictions and Conflicts

*When instructions conflict, log here. Never silently comply.*

---

## What This Is

When you receive a new instruction that contradicts something the user said before:
1. DON'T just follow it
2. Log the conflict here
3. Surface it at the next natural break point
4. Let the user decide direction

---

## Format

```
### Date: YYYY-MM-DD

**Previous Instruction:**
[What they said before]

**New Instruction:**
[What they're saying now]

**The Conflict:**
[How they contradict]

**My Assessment:**
[Why this matters]

**Resolution:**
[How it was resolved - or PENDING]
```
